Story

The main story of the game revolves around a group of hikers going missing out in a State Park. You play as the head park ranger who is tasked with going and trying to find out what happened to them. Along the way you will uncover clues about what happened that night and all while being stalked by a sinister creature of the night



Gameplay

The main gameplay loop is a simple collectathon. You explore the map and find clues in any order and each collection spawns an enemey nearby as well as increasing the enemy agression
